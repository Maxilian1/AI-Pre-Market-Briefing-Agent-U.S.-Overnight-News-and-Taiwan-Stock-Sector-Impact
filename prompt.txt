Research framing:
A signal is generated before the Taiwan market opens for a Taiwan trading date D.
Valid return outcomes for date D include:
- prev_close_to_open_return = Open_D / Close_previous_trading_day - 1
- open_to_close_return = Close_D / Open_D - 1
- close_to_close_return = Close_D / Close_previous_trading_day - 1
- next_close_to_close_return = Close_next_trading_day / Close_D - 1

These returns are labels for research validation only. They must not be used in signal generation.

- Do not drop rows without returns; mark them unavailable.
- Do not look ahead for signal construction. Return labels can include next_close_to_close_return as an outcome label only.
